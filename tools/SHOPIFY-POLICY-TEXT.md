## Return and refund policy

> **Every item is printed on demand, just for you.**
>
> **Wrong, damaged or misprinted?** Contact us within 30 days of delivery
> with your order number and a photo, and you'll get a free replacement or a
> full refund — your choice. Fastest way: trymstene.com/contact
>
> **Lost in transit?** If your order never arrives, contact us within 30 days
> of the estimated delivery date and we'll send a replacement or refund you.
>
> **Change of mind:** because each product is made to order (and custom
> designs are personal to you), made-on-demand items are exempt from the
> 14-day withdrawal right for distance sales (EU Directive 2011/83/EU,
> art. 16(c) — goods made to the consumer's specifications). If something
> still feels wrong with your order, write to us anyway — we're a one-person
> banana operation and we'll figure it out.
>
> **Sizing:** garments are made to order, so please check the size guide
> before purchasing — size-swap returns aren't possible.
>
> **Custom orders** are reviewed by hand before printing. If a design can't
> be printed, you receive a full refund and nothing is produced.

## Shipping policy

> **Everything is made after you order it** — no warehouse, no overstock.
>
> - **Production:** typically 2–5 business days.
> - **Delivery:** typically 5–20 business days after production, depending
>   on your region. You'll get a tracking link by email when it ships.
> - **Custom stickers & magnets** (made in the banana builder at
>   trymstene.com/make-a-banana): **free shipping worldwide**.
> - **Shop products** (apparel, mugs, posters, totes): shipping is
>   calculated at checkout based on your address.
> - Prices include tax where applicable. Orders shipped to some countries
>   may be subject to import duties set by local customs.
