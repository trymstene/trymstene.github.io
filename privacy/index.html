privacy & cookies

No accounts, no ads, no selling data. Here's the whole story.

What gets counted

  • Google Analytics — counts visits and which pages people like, so I know whether anyone actually uses the things I build. In Europe this only runs after you say yes to the cookie banana.
  • Microsoft Clarity — anonymized replays of how pages get used (where taps miss, where layouts confuse). Same rule: Europe only after a yes.
  • Cloudflare Analytics — a cookieless visit counter. It sets nothing on your device and identifies no one, so it runs for everyone.
  • The shop checkout runs on Shopify, which has its own privacy policy and handles payment details — I never see card numbers.

What stays in your browser

Your built banana, bananacoins, badges, and pass live in your browser's own storage (localStorage) — that's your save file, not tracking. It never leaves your device unless you create a passkey to sync it, and even then it's only readable by you and me.
