What is this?

In 1999 I drew a dancing banana and it became one of the most-shared GIFs on the internet. This gallery is the banana's official meme collection — every GIF and sticker here is made by me, its original creator, and every one is free. Transparent-background bananas are stickers (they float over any chat), captioned ones are GIFs.

Looking for the community's versions instead? Browse the community remix gallery — 266 bananas drawn by the internet since 1999. Want yours printed? The banana builder puts your design on real stickers and tees.
